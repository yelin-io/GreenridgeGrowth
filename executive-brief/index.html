A Note on M365 Copilot
We are aware that M365 Copilot offers native capabilities for some of the day-to-day tasks your team handles, such as email triage, meeting summaries, and single-document analysis. Our engagement is focused on the work that M365 Copilot cannot do: reasoning across your full portfolio, connecting systems like DealCloud, HubSpot, and QuickBooks into unified workflows, and building the AI operating model that makes a 15-person team perform like a much larger one.
Why Phase 3 Comes Last
The AI platforms -- Anthropic, OpenAI, Google -- are shipping new native connectors and capabilities on a near-weekly basis. Every week of custom development we defer has a real chance of being replaced by out-of-the-box functionality that is more reliable and more maintainable. Phases 1 and 2 give us time to evaluate the integration landscape and lock in the right architecture before committing investment to custom work that could become redundant.
