02 -- Phase 1 Engagement

Two Parallel Tracks.
One Clear Commitment.

Five-to-six-week Phase 1 restructured per your April 10 framing. Track 1 delivers foundational AI training to the full team -- the bulk of the work. Track 2 produces a Phase 2 scope collaboratively, timed late so the team's training-driven capability gains shape the ideas. Phase 2 is intentionally not pre-specified; it emerges from Phase 1.

Track 1 -- Foundational Training
Team Enablement Across the Full 15-Person Firm
Seven half-day sessions shaped for your team's existing Claude fluency. Foundations compressed, Cowork deep-dive added, role-specific cohorts for sourcing and portfolio operations, and a Closing Applied Workshop. Roughly two to three half-days per week -- learn without stepping away from deals.
Track 2 -- Phase 2 Discovery
Scope the Future State, Collaboratively
One to two late-phase discovery sessions with you plus the Yelin.io team. Produces a Phase 2 SOW draft with two or three priced options -- grounded in what the team actually learned during training.

Inside the Discovery & Training Plan
Our homework and your training blueprint in one document. Five sections, delivered Week 3.
Section 1
Discovery Findings
What we heard in five to six interviews with you plus reps from sourcing, portfolio ops, and deal execution. Workflow reality, stuck points, current tooling, what would move the needle. Grounds everything else in the Plan in what's actually happening at GGP today.
Section 2
Cowork Connectors & Integration Playbook
Out-of-the-box connectors and known MCPs for GGP's stack -- DealCloud, HubSpot, M365, OneNote, Salesloft, Clay. Setup instructions your team can execute without writing code. Anything that requires custom development is Phase 2 territory. Phase 1 stays on Cowork + Claude core functionality. GGP walks away able to manage and extend their own connectors.
Section 3
Security + Governance Guidelines
Cowork usage model mapped to GGP's risk posture (MNPI, portfolio financials, live deal data). Skill publishing controls and review workflow. Anthropic Trust Center findings (SOC 2, data retention, residency) packaged for partner-level review. GGP-specific Acceptable Use Guidelines derived from Anthropic's Usage Policies.
Section 4
Public AI Adoption Signals in PE
Curated digest of publicly disclosed AI initiatives at comparable PE firms -- Blackstone, KKR, Vista, Apollo announcements; Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google customer case studies; BVP, McKinsey, and Axios Pro Rata coverage. Synthesis of public sources for context -- not proprietary access.
Section 5 · The Training Blueprint
Tailored Training Curriculum
The actual content for the seven half-day sessions -- topics, exercises, prompts, examples -- built from the Discovery Findings (Section 1) and Anthropic's AI Fluency 4Ds framework. Compressed where your team is already fluent; deeper on Cowork where the value is newest. Not a generic curriculum. Every example, demo, and exercise reflects GGP's own workflows as surfaced in the interviews.
Training Structure
Training is delivered as half-day sessions only so your team can fit learning around their day-jobs. Seven half-days total across Weeks 3-5, shaped for your intermediate-to-advanced Claude fluency. Structure: a kickoff half-day opens Week 3; three whole-team sessions follow -- Delegation + Description compressed for a team that already prompts daily, then Discernment + Diligence as a full session on evaluation and MNPI/compliance guardrails, then a dedicated Cowork deep-dive on skills, projects, and advanced features. Two half-day role cohorts follow (sourcing and portfolio operations), and a Closing Applied Workshop at the end of Week 5 where the team applies to their own work. Grounded in Anthropic's AI Fluency framework -- source material we build from, not courseware we send you to.
